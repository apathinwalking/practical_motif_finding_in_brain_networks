My St. Mary's Project

Practical Motif Finding in Brain Networks

My St. Mary's Project was a research project that I performed from Spring 2012 through Spring 2013. In it, I studied the methodological limits of "network motif" discovery in large brain networks. In the paper, I explored limits, and possibilities for better algorithms for motif discovery. Using methodology I learned in my time researching motif discovery, I was able to find a a significant motif, found in differing frequencies between children's structural brain networks with and without ADHD.
